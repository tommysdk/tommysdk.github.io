I'm a software professional who is specialized on developing quality software running on the Java platform. I'm an advocate of open source software, test-driven development, Continuous Delivery, DevOps, automation and tools that boost developer productivity. I've also contributed to various open source projects during my spare time, such as Arquillian, ShrinkWrap and Apache TomEE. Currently a maintainer of the Jenkins Delivery Pipeline plugin. I like to try out new technologies and to constantly learn new things.


I speak regurlarly on software conferences and I've also held lectures on test-driven development, Continuous Delivery and Docker for software engineering students at the Royal Institute of Technology. Co-founder and organizer of the Continuous Delivery Stockholm user group with over 400 members.


I'm currently working as a consultant at Diabol in Stockholm, Sweden. If you're interested in hiring me for contract work, on-site lectures, presentations, workshops etc, please contact me on LinkedIn.
