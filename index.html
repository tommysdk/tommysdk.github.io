I'm specializing on developing Java EE based solutions and systems running on the Java platform. I'm an advocate of open source software, test driven development, Continuous Delivery, automation and tools that boosts developer productivity. I also contribute to various open source projects during my spare time, such as JBoss Arquillian & ShrinkWrap. I like to try out new technologies and products that becomes available on the market and to constantly learn new things.


I'm an occasional conference speaker and I've also held a lecture on test driven development for software engineering students at the Royal Institute of Technology.


I'm currently working as a consultant at Diabol in Stockholm, Sweden. If you're interested in hiring me for contract work, on-site lectures, workshops etc, please contact me on LinkedIn.
